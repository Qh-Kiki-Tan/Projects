Figma Prototypes

Co-Hop

Co-Hop, a mobile app designed to address the unpredictable nature of co-op job placements, leading to frequent, last-minute housing searches. Co-Hop solves this by providing a dedicated platform for co-op students to find housing.

Key features include:
  • Targeted Housing Search: Focuses on co-op work terms and study periods.
  • Verified Listings & Peer-to-Peer: Eliminates third-party involvement for direct interaction.
  • Secure In-App Chat: Facilitates communication between users.
  • Community Page: Allows students to share tips and connect.

The app's development followed a Design Thinking process, involving user research, ideation, prototyping, and user testing. Feedback led to improvements in typography, interactive elements, and usability.

WasteSort

WasteSort is a mobile app designed to improve restaurant waste management and promote sustainability. The app addresses the problem of inconsistent and inconvenient waste sorting practices in restaurants.

Key features include:
  • QR Code Scanning: Identifies food items and provides sorting instructions.
  • Online Ordering Integration: Reduces wait times.
  • EcoPoints Reward System: Incentivizes sustainable practices.

Through autoethnography and secondary research, the team informed their design and refined the app's features.
