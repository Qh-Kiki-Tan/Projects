Co-Hop

Co-Hop, a mobile app designed to address unpredictable nature of co-op job placements, leading to frequent, last-minute housing searches. Co-Hop solve this by providing a dedicated platform for co-op students to find housing.
Key features include:

  • Targeted Housing Search Focuses on co-op work terms and study periods.
  • Verified Listings & Peer-to-Peer: Eliminates third-party involvement, promoting direct interaction between students and landlords.
  • Secure In-App Chat: Facilitates communication between users.
  • Community Page: Allows students to share tips, advice, and connect with others.

The app's development followed a Design Thinking process, involving user research (interviews), ideation, prototyping (low-fidelity and high-fidelity), and user testing. Feedback led to improvements in typography, interactive elements, and usability.
